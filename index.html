WHAT IS A LAPTOP

A laptop is a portable and electronified device which accepts data through the input device
(e.g Keyboard, Mouse), accepts and processes data through the system unit(S.U)
and brings out the required information through the output unit(E.g Monitor, speaker)

Laptops varies, i.e, they are based on sizes, uses, types, based
on proccessors, usage, form factors, operating e.t.c but,
we will look at each of the co-respective ideas and more, and if you
will like to add more, then you can suggest it into our mailbox.
